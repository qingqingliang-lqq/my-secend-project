What is Monster Survivors?

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face off against waves of monstrous enemies. Your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible while defeating increasingly difficult monsters.

As you progress, you'll collect power-ups, unlock new abilities, and upgrade your character to become stronger. Strategic decision-making is key – choose the right upgrades and position yourself carefully to maximize your chances of survival.

With its addictive gameplay loop and progressive difficulty, Monster Survivors offers hours of challenging entertainment for players of all skill levels.

How to Play

1

Movement

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map, avoiding enemy attacks and positioning strategically.

2

Combat

Your character attacks automatically. Focus on positioning and collecting power-ups to maximize your damage output.

3

Upgrades

Level up by defeating enemies. Each level-up allows you to choose from a selection of upgrades to enhance your abilities.

4

Survival

The longer you survive, the more difficult the enemies become. Plan your upgrades wisely to handle late-game challenges.
